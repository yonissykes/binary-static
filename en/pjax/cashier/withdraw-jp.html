Withdraw

{JAPAN ONLY}You can request a funds transfer to your designated bank account at any time.

{JAPAN ONLY}You must have sufficient balance in your trading account at the time of transfer.

{JAPAN ONLY}Please note the maximum regular withdrawal amount is ¥1,000,000.

{JAPAN ONLY}Please contact support@binary.com if you need to withdraw more than the maximum amount, as further ID verification is required.

{JAPAN ONLY}Please allow up to 5 business days for the funds to be credited to your designated bank account.

{JAPAN ONLY}If your bank account details have changed, please contact Customer Support in order to verify your ID and new account details.

{JAPAN ONLY}Please input the amount you would like to withdraw and press the Request button.
