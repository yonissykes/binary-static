Mengapa Memilih Trading Binary?

Trading binary sangat sederhana.

A binary option is an option with a fixed payout that is determined at the start of the trade. If your prediction is correct, you receive an agreed payout. If not, you lose your initial stake, and no more. The options are called 'binary' because there can be only two outcomes - win or lose.

Trading binary fleksibel.

Dengan Binary.com Anda dapat bertrading:

  • Semua pasar - valuta asing, indeks saham dan komoditas.
  • Semua kondisi pasar - naik/turun, menyentuh/tidak menyentuh, di dalam/di luar.
  • Semua durasi - dari 10 detik hingga 365 hari.
  • Hasil - dari $1 hingga $50,000.
